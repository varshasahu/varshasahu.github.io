Top 10 Things to do in Tarkarli
Lifestyle Gadget
Varsha Sahu

31st Dec, 2017 11:21 am

Looking for a place which has nice and clean beaches, water activities , scuba diving, beautiful sceneries and nearby Pune or Mumbai ? Then TARKARLI is your answer. Tarkarli is an amazing tourist destination along the coast of Konkan. It is located few miles North of Goa, in the Sindhudurg District of Maharashtra State. It is 390kms from Pune and 525 kms from Mumbai.Tarkarli is full of scenic beaches surrounded by hills and lush greenery.

Tarkarli Beach : Visit Tarkarli beach in early morning or evening. This is one of the cleanest beach I have seen. Take a walk on this beach , watch sunrise or sunset and relax. There are lot of staying option near the beach itself. I stayed in one of the accomadation provided by Tarkarli official website. The beach was at the walking distance from my stay.

Rent a bike : The best option to roam around is to have your own vehicle. This would help you a lot to visit the main attractions near Tarkarli, You can easily rent a bike in tarkarli and malvan under 300-350 INR per day. The rates could vary as per the season

Scuba Diving : One of the most amazing things that can be enjoyed in Tarkarli, scuba diving not only introduces you with the mesmeric charm of the marine life, but also make you aware about its importance and conservation. While the presence of expert and certified scuba instructors along with proper safety equipment makes diving much more exciting, the ample of marine life adds more to the experience here. This is one of the cheapest place to do suba diving in maharashtra. I did this in 1100 INR but it generally depends on the season. You can make online booking from Tarkarli Tourism This can be done in both malvan and tarkarli as well.

Malvan Beach : Malvan beach is also a nice beach and it is around 8kms from tarkarli. This is also a great option to stay. There are lot of water activities going on this beach and they are slightly cheaper than the activities on tarkarli beach.

Sindhudurg Fort : This is the must visit place in near tarkarli. You will need to buy tickets to visit this fort. Then a boat will atke you to an small island where this fort is located. I visited this fort in evening and watching the sunset from There was one of the best experience. Try to reach this place at the earliest as there is lot of rush and lot of time get wasted in waiting in line for tickets. The fort closes around 6 PM and all the visitors must board their boats before this time to come back to the malvan. Normally it will take around 2-3 Hours to visit this but the view would be worth it.

Dolphin Spotting : Spot these lively creatures swimming along the beaches or in the deep sea and witness them searching for food; make sure you are all prepared to capture them on your lenses and make your dolphin cruise more exciting and memorable!

Try Sol Kadi : Sol ladi is a malvan drink which as coconut in it and looks pink in colour. This is really refreshing drink. You can easily find it anywhere in restaurants. The locals makes it and you should definitely try it once.

Para Sailing and Water Sports : For those who love water sports, Tarkarli is a good option. Lot of water sports are offered here like para sailing, jet ski, banana ride and many more.

Ride a bike on Beach : Riding a bike on beach in evening can be a good experience.

Backwaters of Karli River : The scenic beauty of Backwaters at Tarkarli is beyond imagination. Beautiful Virgin beaches surrounded by lush green trees, fresh cool breeze flowing around you every second makes you forget all your worries and have a time of your life
