Top 10 Things to do in Dubai
Lifestyle Gadget
Varsha Sahu

31st Dec, 2017 11:21 am

From world’s famous “Burj Khalifa” to “Palm Jumeirah”. Here are the top 10 things that you must see if you are in Dubai.

The Dubai Mall : It is right beside the Burj Khalifa and is the largest mall in the world by total area.The best part about it that other than designer shops, multi cuisine restaurants, there are other things that you can see in this mall without even spending a penny and would be worth watching. Such as Dubai Mall Aquarium, The Gold Souk, Dubai Mall Waterfall


Palm Jumeirah This is one of the three man-made islands in Dubai.The scale of development is astounding, the vision is amazing.You will see Atlantis hotel on the way to Jumeirah island which is a great example of Dubai’s Beauty.

The JBR Walk and Dubai Marina : The walk at Jumeirah beach Residence in Dubai is the place where you can simply enjoy by walking alongside the beach. You can find nice restaurants, sports shoe shops, cafes nearby.This whole city looks different in the night. So my suggestion would be to visit this place at night also.

Dubai Mall Musical Fountain Show : This 15-20 minutes Fountain Show takes place at Dubai Mall every hour in evening in front of Burj Khalifa.This is something you don’t want to miss. It starts at 7:00 pm and takes place hour and timings are displayed on a board.The way these Fountains and music is synchronised will literally blow your mind.


Global Village Festival This is one of the most exciting things to do as you get to see various countries pavilions consisting of shopping stalls, cultural shows, authentic food stalls, dance performances, games, everywhere is an attraction. Great place to take pictures with famous things around the world like an Egyptian mummy, Russian dolls, London bus, Eiffel tower etc. As I like to call it as “Mini world Tour” which one must experience here.

Dubai Miracle Garden This garden justifies its name completely. It is a flower garden which has beautiful landscape designs(aeroplane, pretty houses) all covered with millions of colourful flowers.A really nice place to go with family.You will get the feeling of Disney world.

Dubai Water Canal You haven’t been to the Canal if you haven’t seen the water cascade along the sides of Shaikh Zayed Road. That being said, the best time to visit the Canal are between 8-10pm when the water starts flowing. B Fun Fact: If any water transport passes through the waterfall, the water ‘opens up’ and makes way for the craft.

Laser Show at Dubai Festival City Mall A show like no other! This sound and light show starts with the projection of lady dancing on the fountain with some soothing music going on in a background and ends with mind blowing fireball effects in water.This show would you feel like you are in a movie with graphics.

Glow Garden (Zabeel Park) Dubai Garden Glow is a unique park and very winsome. It’s beauty is par excellence. It feels like I have returned to my childhood as if I was in wonderland. There were glowing life-sized dinosaurs like those in Jurassic Park which are amazing.Cute glowing pandas, ants, flowers. A Very adorable place to go with family.

Desert Safari You will need to have a whole day to go for desert safari. The club buses will pick you up from your pick up location and take you the desert location. Only 15 minutes desert safari part was fun.Other than that you can enjoy Belly Dancing, camel riding.I personally didn’t like the food provided by them.
